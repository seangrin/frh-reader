Description

Gabriel Voss is an alchemically grown heavy created in Eastern Europe more than a century ago, now living as a quiet protector in the margins of the Followers of the Right Hand. He does not breathe, eat, or sleep, and his body can absorb terrible punishment, but that toughness is not free: torn flesh and damaged mass must later be rebuilt in privacy with sutures, supplies, occult treatment, and patient workshop care. His dead maker left only fragmentary journals, so Gabriel still does not know whether he was intended to be a son, servant, bodyguard, replacement, or merely proof that such a thing could be done.

Gabriel keeps a sewing kit filled with sutures and field-repair supplies. His heavier flesh loss is intended to be repaired later at base with time, treatment, and occult support rather than assumed to recover automatically.
