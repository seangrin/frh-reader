How This Campaign Is Run

The Game Master provides the world, its inhabitants, and the consequences of the investigators' actions. The story itself is not prewritten. Instead, it emerges from the investigators' choices, theories, and methods.

Players are encouraged to ask questions, pursue leads, and experiment with solutions. Investigations often reward curiosity, research, professional expertise, and creative thinking as much as combat ability.

The world reacts logically to what investigators discover and do.

What strange mystery would make you investigate?
