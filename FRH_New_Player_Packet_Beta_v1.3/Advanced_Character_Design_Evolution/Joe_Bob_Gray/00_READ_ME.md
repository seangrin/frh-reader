# Joe Bob Gray

This subfolder holds the Joe Bob Gray extreme-example build files.

Joe Bob is not a standard onboarding pregen. He is preserved here as an advanced Southern Gothic concept line and cautionary example.

Contents:

- human form sheet
- werewolf form sheet
- related advanced-evolution material
- GM-facing engineering note for the `2026-03-20` `Shapeshifters` correction

Use him when you want:
