# Joe Bob Gray: Southern Gothic Extreme Example

Joe Bob Gray is not a standard FRH onboarding pregen.

He sits in `Advanced_Character_Design_Evolution\Joe_Bob_Gray` because he is useful as:

- an example of an extreme character concept
- an example of a split-form build
- an example of how Southern Gothic can be pushed hard inside FRH

## Why He Is Here

Joe Bob is mechanically and tonally unusual:

- separate human and werewolf sheets
- transformation pressure
- GM-controlled loss of agency in werewolf form
- a much harsher social and moral profile than the core pregens

That makes him valuable as a design example, but risky as a default new-player option.

## Use Case

Use Joe Bob when:

- a player explicitly wants a darker Southern Gothic concept
- you want to show how far FRH can stretch without changing systems
- you want an advanced example of how secrecy, curse-pressure, and split identity can be built

Do not present Joe Bob as a normal first-pass pregen unless that tone is intentional.
