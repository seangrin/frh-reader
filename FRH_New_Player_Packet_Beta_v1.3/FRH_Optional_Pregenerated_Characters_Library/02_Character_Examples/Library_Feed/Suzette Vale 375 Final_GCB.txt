Description
New Orleans-born mourning-house proprietor and occult mortuary consultant. Suzette is quietly consulted by funeral directors, embalmers, clergy, cemetery staff, and medical examiners when a body is difficult, a rite is obscure, or the dead feel wrong. Secretly she is a disciplined vampire tolerated by a narrow Kindred compact because she is discreet, useful, and deeply embedded in the hidden administration of death.
