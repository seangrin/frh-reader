  1. Occult Investigators Wanted

1923 Supernatural Mystery Campaign

GURPS 3e • Investigation • Strange Mysteries


A tabletop role-playing campaign set in 1923 America, investigating strange events, occult rumors, and unexplained phenomena.

Players portray members of a secret organization known as The Followers of the Right Hand, attempting to determine whether disturbing reports are fraud, coincidence…or something supernatural.

Investigations may involve:

• interviewing witnesses
• researching historical records
• examining unusual artifacts
• uncovering hidden motives
• confronting things that do not behave according to known rules

Understanding the problem is often more effective than attacking it.

Careful observation and creative reasoning often reveal solutions others miss.

Tone inspirations include:

Kolchak: The Night Stalker
The Twilight Zone
The X-Files
Fringe
early Hellboy

This campaign rewards curious, thoughtful players who enjoy solving mysteries and exploring strange ideas.

Scheduling

Sessions will run approximately once per month Saturday evenings (around 7–11 PM PT).

Games will likely be hybrid:

• some players in person
• others joining via Zoom

Adult schedules are understood. Characters may occasionally be away on other assignments if a player is unable to attend.

System

GURPS (rules included in the packet)
No previous GURPS experience required.
