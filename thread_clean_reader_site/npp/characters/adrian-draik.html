Description

A hardboiled private investigator who passes for human only while his glamour amulet holds. Under the fedora and trench coat, Adrian is an infernal-blooded Tiefling sorcerer with a talent for smoke, lies, fire, and reading bad rooms before they get worse. He works occult cases for FRH and keeps one hand near the missing amulet because if the glamour fails in front of the wrong witnesses, he stops being a detective and becomes a specimen.
