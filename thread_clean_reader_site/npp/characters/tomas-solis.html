Description

Semi-retired but still quietly trusted by Treasury-connected superiors, Tomas Solis has been sent West to keep discreet watch on strange federal behavior and deliver a personal report through back channels. In briefings he says little, leans against the wall, and listens until the whole shape of the matter settles into place. He does not think of himself as chosen, only responsible. His gift is not spectacle but intervention: in moments of crisis, invisible solar force turns aside harm meant for other people.
