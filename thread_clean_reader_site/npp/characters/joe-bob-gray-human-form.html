Description

Joe Bob works as a PI in New Orleans. From an early age, he learned he was cursed to uncontrollabley turn into a mindless werewolf when in danger or angry. He has managed to hide that along with his membership in the SSS and his hatred of non-whites. He keeps his mouth shut while he tries to find a cure to his lycanthropy.
