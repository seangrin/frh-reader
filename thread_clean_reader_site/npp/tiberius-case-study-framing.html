Tiberius Sinclair – Catastrophic Knowledge Exposure

Background

During an investigation for the Followers of the Right Hand (FRH), Tiberius Sinclair uncovered a partial folio of De Vermis Mysteriis. This was part of the FRH's occult research into irregularities in maritime cargo and occult financial transactions.

Despite the FRH protocol, which mandates psychic containment, buffering, and supervised readings of such dangerous materials, Tiberius chose to read the text privately and unprepared.

What followed was not a summoning, a gate-opening, or a manifested entity — but a waking intrusion:

  • Yellow curtains.

  • Electric light hum.

  • Smell of ink.

  • A bookmark resting in the page.

In less than a minute, his perception fused with a metaphysical presence, leaving him psychologically shattered.

Mechanical Outcome

Tiberius experienced a catastrophic cascade of Fright and Derangement, leading to a loss of his faculties and abilities far beyond normal limits. The mechanical outcome was:

  • -490 points in disadvantages

  • Extensive phobic fragmentation

  • Substance addiction

  • Combat Paralysis

  • Total Pacifism

  • Social Withdrawal (Reclusive)

  • Delusional Severance from Psionic Abilities

  • Compulsive Destruction of Written Material

His effective character value was reduced from 375 points to 25 points. While his attributes and advantages remain on paper, they are functionally unusable.

Current Status

Tiberius Sinclair is permanently retired from play.
He is now institutionalized and alive, but will not return to active gameplay.

However, his story serves as a demonstration of the consequences of exposure to forbidden knowledge. He is not a mere cautionary tale about recklessness, but a testament to the fundamental danger of knowing too much.

Tiberius may make appearances as an NPC, but he will never return to active play.
