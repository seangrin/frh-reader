This is the reader's front door. The intended experience is simple: read the intro, then move through Sessions 1-8 in order. The cast page and reference gallery are there as support material you can consult before reading or dip into as needed while moving forward.

All conversational turns are preserved. Cleanup was limited to presentation, encoding repair where possible, and heading structure so the material stays readable and imports more cleanly into word processors.

Main Reading Workflow

  1. Intro and Setup - the onboarding runway into the campaign, Sean's GM philosophy, and the pre-record situation.
  2. Sessions 1-8 - read forward using each session page's `Previous` and `Next` links.
  3. Postscript and Analysis - the closeout material after Session 8.

Reference While Reading

  • Cast at a Glance - the fastest way to meet the party in prose.
  • Reference Gallery - portraits, maps, cosmology images, source documents, and character sheets.
  • GMA Curated Session Segments - converted portable review corpus with session context, segment transcripts, manifests, and review notes.
  • Contents - a chapter-level overview if you want a wider map of the packet.
  • Assembly Notes - mainly useful if you are turning the packet into a Writer document.

Best use: browser reading first, Writer assembly later.
