Review Protocol

Goal

After reading a segment, produce critique that helps train the GMA on Sean's actual style rather than abstract GM theory.

Review Structure

Use the matching review file sections:

  1. What The GMA Read Correctly
  2. What The GMA Misread
  3. GM Technique Notes
  4. NPC Handling Notes
  5. Table Culture Notes
  6. Do Not Over-Generalize
  7. Carry Forward

Review Rules

  • Anchor claims to specific line IDs whenever possible.
  • Distinguish GM behavior from NPC behavior.
  • Distinguish style from content.
  • Distinguish one-off quirks from repeatable techniques.

Valuable Critique Types

  • soft pressure versus hard pressure
  • implication versus explicit statement
  • when Sean lets consequences stand
  • when Sean rescues pacing
  • when table banter is part of trust and culture rather than noise
  • how novice-player sidewaysness is absorbed or redirected
  • how major NPCs teach through diction and brevity

Bad Review Habits

  • vague praise with no textual anchor
  • calling everything a “lesson” without saying what the lesson is
  • mistaking NPC exposition for pure lore dump when it is actually tone or leverage
  • treating table chaos as unusable noise

Strong Review Example Pattern

  • Correct: At S04-03-259, the GMA correctly noticed that Kobayashi uses etiquette as pressure, not politeness for its own sake.
  • Missed: At S03-04-419, the GMA failed to notice that Mr. Smoke's casino metaphor compresses both uncertainty and leverage into one brief line.
  • Carry Forward: When Sean uses a polished NPC, brevity often carries more authority than extra explanation.
