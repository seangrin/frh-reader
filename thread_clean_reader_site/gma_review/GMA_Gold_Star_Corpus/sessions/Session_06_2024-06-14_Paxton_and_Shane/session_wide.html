Corpus Intent

  • Use this session packet for both apprenticeship-style GMA training and DR recovery.
  • Session note: Breakout session emphasizing reconnaissance, planning, and theological / infernal implications around the holy nail.

Major NPC Registry

Kobayashi

  • Tier: major
  • Function: recurring criminal liaison / clue authority
  • Significance: Even off-screen, Kobayashi's exact wording and promised follow-up drive the whole holy-nail search and the post-retrieval plan.
  • Performance notes: Usually present here through quoted or relayed language rather than long direct speech; keep him distinct from Mr. Smoke.

Hotel_Concierge

  • Tier: major
  • Function: elite logistics / occult-networking facilitator
  • Significance: The concierge becomes the concrete social gateway to knife-makers, domino masks, and the Brimstone Collective.
  • Performance notes: Smooth, transactional, delighted to solve rich-people problems without visible friction.

Basilica_Priest

  • Tier: minor
  • Function: sacred gatekeeping presence
  • Significance: Represents the human-facing authority layer around the basilica and keeps the church from reading as empty scenery.
  • Performance notes: Non-speaking presence only. The priest does not deliver dialogue in this session. Functions as visual and atmospheric gatekeeping — his physical presence signals active human occupation of the sacred space, not exposition. No overlay lines.
