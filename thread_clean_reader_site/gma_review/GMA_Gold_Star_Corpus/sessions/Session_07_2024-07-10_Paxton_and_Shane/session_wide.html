Major NPC Registry

Anita

  • Tier: major
  • Function: prophetic pressure / revelation trigger
  • Significance: Anita's staged spiritualism turning into real revelation is one of the most important live-NPC pivots in the breakout corpus.
  • Performance notes: Begins in cold-reading spiritualist patter, then abruptly becomes dangerous once real mana and heavenly attention break through. Her overlay block (S07-03-289 through S07-03-303) is her describing the cherub's appearance: muscular human body with lion's hands and feet, bull's face, eagle's wings — she is delivering a vision, not social dialogue. The S07-04 scene title "Anita's halo revelation" refers to the halo that subsequently appears on Crispin as witnessed by the room, not to further Anita speech. Anita has no overlay lines in S07-04.

White_Masked_Host

  • Tier: major
  • Function: Brimstone social framing / damage control
  • Significance: The host defines the room's social contract, introduces Anita, and instantly reframes disruption back into decadent applause.
  • Performance notes: Polished, theatrical, socially dominant, and always steering the room rather than reacting honestly.

demonlings

  • Tier: minor
  • Function: infernal chaos vector / unreliable security
  • Significance: The demonlings frame the whole hotel's risk profile and later become a practical cleanup problem.
  • Performance notes: Petty, opportunistic, and more capable than Preston initially hopes.

Basilica_Priest

  • Tier: minor
  • Function: sacred witness
  • Significance: The priest who sees the cross appear on the altar helps ground the heavenly lane in ordinary human perception.
  • Performance notes: Non-speaking. Reacts as a human witness to miracle-adjacent intrusion — his physical reaction (arms raised during mass, chalice aloft, congregation's blind spot) is the scene beat, not delivered dialogue. One overlay line in S07-06 via text_inferred method.
